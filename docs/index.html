Team #017 BoxCoxRox Multifaceted Product Recommendation System

Mouse over the different product bubbles to discover the perfect product for your favorite pet. Click on a bubble to zoom in. While zoomed in, click again to zoom out. The size of the bubble reflects the number of reviews for each product, while color represents the average sentiment of reviews for that product. The best products in each category and topic are indicated with stars. Finally, click on the link in the tooltip to be redirected to Amazon.com to see the product. For more information, please see the project repo on GitHub.
