Team #017 BoxCoxRox Multifaceted Product Recommendation Engine

We used multiple machine learning and natural language processing techniques to analyze over 5 million reviews of pet products from Amazon.com to help you find just what you are looking for. Mouse over the different product categories at the right to discover what's best for you. The size of the bubble reflects the number of people that have reviewed the product. The lighter the color, the more positive the review. Products with the stars are ranked as the very best in their particular category and topic. Finally, click on the link in the tooltip to be redirected to Amazon.com to see the product!
